About Resume

Hello, I’m Yenny,
UX and Visual designer based in San Francisco Bay Area.

As a UX/UI Desginer and former buisness owner, I have a great wealth of experience in both sides of the buisness. I believe that UX design is the foundational bridge between products and users. As a designer, we have the power to empathize with the user and develop a seamless and enjoyable experience that also achieves business goals. When users are pleased, and can interact effiently, it will result in competitive and profitable products. Successful user experience requires solving problems that will benefit all parties.

Travelling is one of my favorite pastimes. Experiencing different cultures and international cuisines are what I live for. I also enjoy playing and making games. I've participated in several game hackathons, where the goal is to create a fun and playable game within 72 hours. When I’m not travelling, designing or playing games, I love to craft. I make jewelry, books, and leather goods such as handbags and wallets.

Thanks for visiting viewing my portfolio,

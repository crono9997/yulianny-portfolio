About Me

Hello, I’m Yenny,
UX and Visual designer based in San Francisco Bay Area.

I am originally from Borneo Island and grew up in Jakarta, Indonesia. I earned my BFA degree in Graphic Design from Academy of Art University and my UX Design Immersive from General Assembly.

I’ve always been curious about inconveniences or difficulties with daily tasks that I encounter in my personal life. So naturally I ask myself, why a particular problem exists, what causes it and how it can be solved. I tend to look for solutions in my mind and once refine them over and over until I end up with the best solution, that will not be costly and also benefit everyone. Without even realizing I’ve been exercising user experience all this time.

Traveling is one of my favorite pastimes. I love traveling, so I can experience different cultures and international cuisine. Everytime I travel, just like everybody else I pass through TSA and always ask myself, how this process can be sped up without jeopardizing safety of travellers.

In addition to that, I also play some computer games. My favorite video game is Diablo 3. I also participate in game hackathon, known as “Ludum Dare”. My main task within our team is to design and illustrate art assets within 72 hours.
